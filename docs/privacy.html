MIRROR RUNNERS

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 6, 2026

Overview

Mirror Runners is a mobile game developed by tmmr. We respect your privacy and are committed to transparency about data practices.

Data Collection

Mirror Runners does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All game data (highscores, settings) is stored locally on your device only.

Advertising

Mirror Runners displays interstitial ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. You can remove all ads permanently through an in-app purchase.

In-App Purchases

Mirror Runners offers a one-time in-app purchase to remove ads. Purchases are processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). We do not have access to your payment information.

Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob - Advertising (Privacy Policy)
  • Apple / Google - In-App Purchases

Children's Privacy

Mirror Runners does not knowingly collect data from children. The ads shown are not personalized.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
