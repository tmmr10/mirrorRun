One tap. Two runners. Zero margin for error.

Mirror Runners is a split-screen endless runner that challenges your brain like no other. You control two characters at once — mirrored on each side of a glowing neon line. Swipe to dodge, but every move affects both sides. What saves you on the left might kill you on the right.

THINK FAST. REACT FASTER.
Obstacles fly at you from above — walls, spikes, double barriers, and shifters that change lanes mid-flight. The speed ramps up relentlessly. One hit and it's over.

YOUR BRAIN VS. THE MIRROR
Just when you think you've got the rhythm, the game throws curveballs:
- Mirror Swap — controls flip completely. Left is right. Right is left. Good luck.
- Phantom Mode — your runners fade out. Navigate blind for a heart-stopping moment.
